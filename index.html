Data Analysis - Application and Presentation

Question

What are the top 10 data science jobs based on annual salary?

Data

To answer this question, I used the "Data Science Job Salaries" dataset from Kaggle. The dataset includes information on 606 data scientists from primarily the U.S., but also other countries. The data was aggregated from ai-jobs.net.

Process

After downloading the data in a CSV format, I wrote a Python script that reads the data from the CSV file into JobListing objects. The script then extracts job titles and salaries and calculates the average salary of all jobs with the same title. The bar chart was created using Matplotlib, a Python library for data visualization.

Results & Analysis

The graphic below shows the 10 highest paying job titles in data science.

From the graph, it is evident that amongst the top salaries, leadership positions such as "Data Analytics Lead" and "Principle Data Scientist" receive the highest salaries, while analysts receive the lowest. Data architects and various types of data scientists fall in between.

This information suggests a hierarchy in terms of salaries within the field of data analysis. Leadership positions are at the top of the hierarchy, probably because they hold the most responsibility and require the most extensive skillset. It is also possible that the higher salaries reflect the limited pool of qualified candidates. Data architects and different types of data scientists also receive a relatively high salary, likely due to the specialized skills and knowledge required for these roles.
